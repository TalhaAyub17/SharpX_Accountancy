About Us

At SharpX, we provide a wide range of specialized Accounting, Tax & Advisory services tailored to meet your business needs. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing customized solutions that address the unique challenges and opportunities of each business..

Bookkeeping VAT & CIS Returns

Our Bookkeeping Services provide accurate daily, monthly, and quarterly financial support to help businesses maintain organized and up-to-date records. We manage transaction recording, bank reconciliations, expense tracking, as well as the preparation and submission of VAT and CIS returns, ensuring full compliance with statutory requirements and timely filings.

Self-Assessment

We specialize in the preparation and submission of Self-Assessment tax returns for individuals, sole traders, and partnerships, ensuring full compliance. Our services include income declarations, property income, dividends, interest, Capital Gains Tax computations, expense calculations, partnership tax returns, and MTD ITSA quarterly submissions.

Monthly/Annual Accounts & Payroll

Our Accounts Services provide accurate financial reporting, comprehensive account preparation, and timely submission of annual accounts and Corporation Tax returns. We specialize in managing monthly management accounts for clients who require consistent, up-to-date financial reporting with accuracy and reliability. In addition, our services include payroll processing, PAYE administration, pension management, and RTI submissions, ensuring your business remains fully compliant while maintaining efficient and streamlined financial operations.

Professional Advisory

We provide comprehensive advisory services for individuals and corporations, tailored to their specific needs and financial objectives. Our experts offer strategic guidance and practical solutions to help clients make informed decisions and choose the most effective options for sustainable financial growth. With a client-focused approach and professional expertise, our team is always available to provide support and advice, giving you confidence and peace of mind in managing your financial matters.
